The coolest pyramids of Egypt

Built in the 24th century for the Egyptian pharaoh Unas, it is the oldest Old Kingdom pyramid. It was originally built at a height of 43 m, which is now in a dilapidated condition. It is a very significant pyramid as it represents the first of pyramids to have hymns carved on its wall. It was rediscovered in the 19th century, when a mummy was recovered from the ruins
